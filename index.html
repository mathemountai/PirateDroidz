▸ Fleet Registry

Two Ships. Twenty Floors.

Override hostile droids across two government fleet ships. Board the GFS Anchovy, fight your way up 10 floors, find the escape pod — then breach the GFS Sardine for 10 more floors of pure chaos.

GFS-001
GFS Anchovy
Floors 1–10

The starting vessel. Standard grid layouts with increasing enemy density. Collect floor dots, grab KEY tiles to unlock portals, and fight your way to the top.

  • Classic rectangular floor layouts
  • Escalating droid power (100→999)
  • NEON ⬡ dots refuel your power core
  • Floor 10: Escape Pod portal to Sardine
GFS-002
GFS Sardine
Floors 11–20

The Sardine's donut-shaped floor architecture hides a deadly secret — black hole mechanics that warp droid movement. Only the highest-power override survives.

  • Donut-ring floor topology
  • Black hole gravity mechanics
  • Hostile elite droids (700+ power)
  • Floor 20: Final override for glory

How to Play

🕹️
Move

Use the on-screen joystick below the game viewport to pilot your droid across each floor.

⚔️
Combat

Collide with enemy droids to engage. Higher PWR wins. Defeated droids drop power boosts.

●
Dots

Eat green floor dots to score points. Clear a floor to maximise your score multiplier.

⬡
NEON Fuel

Yellow NEON hexagon dots refuel your power core by +5 PWR. Keep topped up for harder droids.

🔑
KEY Tiles

Step on a cyan KEY tile to unlock the portal door on that floor. No key — no exit.

🚀
Escape

Reach the ESCAPE POD on floor 10 to board the GFS Sardine. Survive all 20 floors to win.

Data We Collect

PIRATEDROIDZ does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The game runs entirely offline on your device.

Your high score and game progress are saved locally on your device only, using standard iOS local storage. This data never leaves your device.
